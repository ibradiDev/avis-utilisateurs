Due to Maven\textquotesingle{}s design, elements are inherited from the parent POM to the project POM. While most of the inheritance is fine, it also inherits unwanted elements like {\ttfamily \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}license\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}} and {\ttfamily \texorpdfstring{$<$}{<}developers\texorpdfstring{$>$}{>}} from the parent. To prevent this, the project POM contains empty overrides for these elements. If you manually switch to a different parent and actually want the inheritance, you need to remove those overrides.
